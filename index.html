about pika

pika is a coed cooperative community of creative students living in an awesome house.

We pride ourselves in our diversity, welcoming atmosphere, nightly home-made dinners (which you're invited to eat for free any time!), and our penchant for building exciting contraptions, practical and silly.

We are an egalitarian society that believes that everyone can work together to build a vibrant, unique, and self-sufficient community.

Come by the house any time and someone will be around to give a house tour including the roof deck, the firepole, the chickens, the treehouse, and much more to explore.

Every night we have dinner at 7:00, and anyone from the MIT undergraduate community is welcome to come.

Feel free to email pika-questions@mit.edu with questions.
